Intro

NCS short for NonCopyrightSounds was a music group ,and record company organize by Bily Woodford in 2011.
The reason why he formed this organization is to find the noncopyrightmusic while playing games.
NCS upload a viriaty kind of music made by different producer, the main purpose is to provide many services of using noncopyrightmusic at every platform
The main platform where NCS upload is Youtube & Twitch ,the main kind of music which NCS upload is EDM,eletronic,house,trap,melodic.
